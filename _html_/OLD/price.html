Common Questions

What if customers sit down in my business for an extended period of time for the WiFi?

We provide complete control on how long a customer can connect to the WiFi. We can limit access anywhere from 30 minutes to 24 hours based on your businesses needs.

What do I need to get set up?

Set up takes less than 5 minutes and you only need an internet connection. Our router simply plugs into your current internet modem. We do not provide internet but simply help your business gain more value from providing internet access to your customers.

What payment options are available?

Customers are sent an invoice via email every month. Payments can be made using all major credit cards (VISA, MasterCard, American Express).
